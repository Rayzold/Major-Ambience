What you get

Built for the table, not the playlist.

Every feature exists because a GM at a real table needed it. Open the library, fire a stinger, snapshot the moment, crossfade to the next scene — all without breaking eye contact with your players.

Auto‑categorized library

Drop your music folder in. The app sorts thousands of tracks into ten buckets — Combat, Tavern, Horror, Rest, SFX — using the rules a working GM would write.

Scenes you can restore in one tap

Snapshot the current category, soundboard layout, fade, and volumes into a named scene. Tap it next week and the whole table mood comes back.

Soundboard for instant triggers

24 pads across three pages. Drag any track onto a slot, fire it with one click. Pads glow when active, loop on demand, and the music bus ducks automatically.

Crossfade & ducking

Smooth transitions between tracks with a slider. SFX ride over the music while it dips automatically — your stinger never gets buried.

Search every track

Full‑text across the whole library, with filters for grade, length, and category. Find the right cue mid‑combat without breaking flow.

Grade what works

Rate tracks S through F as you use them. The weighted shuffle learns: S tracks come up 6× more than C tracks. F tracks never play again.

The product

Windows now. Phone next.

Three‑pane workspace on the desktop — categories, tracks, now playing — with a persistent transport bar. Mobile is the touch‑first companion: mini‑player, swipe‑up Now Playing, twenty‑four tactile soundboard pads. Same design system, same library, same scenes.

Questions

What GMs actually ask.

Is my music library safe? Do you store any of it?
No. Major Ambience never uploads, hosts, or syncs your audio files. The app reads your local folder, builds an index on your device, and that's it. Even your grades and scenes only sync if you turn on cloud sync (Major tier).
Do I need to be online?
After install, no. Major Ambience runs fully offline. The only online features are cloud sync (optional) and checking for updates.
What music does it work with?
Any MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG, or M4A file you have on your hard drive. Curated packs (Battle Bards, AudioHero, Tabletop Audio downloads, your Spotify rips, your commissioned scores) — all yours.
What if I already paid for Syrinscape or Tabletop Audio?
You can use Major Ambience alongside them. If you've downloaded packs from those services, the auto‑categorizer will sort them straight into the right buckets.
When does the mobile app land?
Q1 2027 — public beta on TestFlight and Play Internal Testing about three months after the Windows launch. Free upgrade for anyone who owns Major.
What about Mac and Linux?
Not at launch. The desktop app is built with Tauri 2, which ships Mac and Linux builds for free — the question is support burden. Likely Q2–Q3 2027 if Windows sales support it.
Refunds?
Yes. 14 days, no questions asked, regardless of where you bought it. Email and we'll handle it.
Is there a subscription option?
No, and there never will be. One‑time purchase is a positioning commitment, not just a price tag.
What about VTT integration (Foundry, Roll20, Owlbear)?
Held for the Epic tier in v2. Major Ambience is architected with a TriggerAPI that VTT plugins will hook into — full details when the v2 plan lands.
