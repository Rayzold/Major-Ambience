13 / 14 · Risks
What could go wrong

Five risks. Five mitigations.

RiskLikelihoodMitigation
Bad creator review tanks launch week Medium Never depend on a single Tier‑1 placement. Diversify to 8 Tier‑2 + 30 Tier‑3.
Apple/Google reject mobile build Medium Submit early. Conservative copy. No copyrighted track names in screenshots.
Subscription competitor cuts price to match Medium Lean harder into ownership messaging — their move sharpens our wedge.
Solo‑dev burnout High 90‑day cycles with rest weeks. Outsource support after month 3.
Refund storms from buggy release Low Phase‑gate releases through alpha cohort. Pause sales on critical bugs.
